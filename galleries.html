Where it never stops raining

Narrow Canyons and Deserts

Twisted sand canyons

A photographers paradise. The canyons and deserts of the mojave receive less than six inches of rain annualy. Native Americans have been living in the land as far back as 1500BC and continue to live there to this day. These images are from a trip living and camping on the road for a month.
