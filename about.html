Hello! I'm Skyler Hughes, a photographer from Oregon currently living in Perth, Australia.

Summary

Born and raised in the Pacific Northwest, where it pukes rain, I recently moved to sunny Western Australia to be with my wonderful wife, Leilah. The people are generous, the beaches to die for, but bloody hell it is remote.

I've been shooting landscape photography for almost a decade. I enjoy whenever I can combine the outdoors with photography. I am a firm believer in quality over quantity and I try to take this approach through photography. I will often wait for hours and return to the same location for days until I capture the perfect light. Then come home and wonder why I wasted so much time on a half decent shot.

I believe in living a sustanaible life through educating others about the outdoors and the leave no trace principles. Pick up your trash asshats.

Design

I've been fortunate enough to live the last 5 years of my life as a Product Designer. Currently I am a designer at Bankwest where I work with some fun aussies and people from all over the world.

I like to think I am a nice person so say hello, drop me a line, or get in touch. I like to collaborate or can talk hours about design and photography.
